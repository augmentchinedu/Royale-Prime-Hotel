About Us

Located in the divided capital city of Cyprus on a private 20-acre estate, Royale Prime Hotel sets the yardstick for luxurious accommodation in Northern Cyprus. Royale Prime Hotel is just a 20 minutes drive from the famous Selimiye Mosque and Buyuk Han (The Great Inn).

Royale Prime Hotel is surrounded by specially grown golden oaks, and of course table and wine grapes, with a spectacular mountain backdrop and breath-taking views of archaeological finds. Be transported to a world of exquisite beauty, splendor, and complete tranquillity at this luxury Nicosia hotel.
